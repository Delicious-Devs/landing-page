About Us

We are Delicious Devs, a team of young high schoolers enthusiastic about science and tech. We came together under a hackathon established by Hack Team 6 to build a website related to food.
After a lot of brainstorming, we came up with the idea of a Search Engine that anyone can use to search for a dish name or its ingredients and they will get to know all the processes involved in preparing that dish.
